BridgeLabz

BridgeLabz is an AWS recognised incubator and is solely focussed on solving tech employability. Over the last 7 years, it has worked with 1000+ top tech companies including Yatra, Capgemini, LafargeHolcim, JDA, UrbanLadder, Fullerton India, and RBL bank. Over 10000+ engineers have been placed from Bridgelabz.
